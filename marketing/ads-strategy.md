# Advertising Strategy — Solaris Argentina

---

## Platforms

### 1. Google Ads (Priority 1)
**Why:** High-intent search traffic
**Budget:** $1,000-2,000/month initial

**Keywords:**
- "paneles solares para empresas argentina"
- "energía solar industrial buenos aires"
- "instalación solar comercial"
- "ahorro energía solar argentina"
- "paneles solares precio argentina"
- "generación distribuida argentina"
- "solar rooftop industrial"

**Ad Groups:**
1. Brand awareness (solar argentina, solaris)
2. Problem-aware (factura eléctrica alta, ahorro energía)
3. Solution-aware (paneles solares, instalación solar)
4. Competitor (vs. enSight, solar alternatives)

**Landing:** solaris-argentina.com with form

### 2. LinkedIn Ads (Priority 2)
**Why:** Decision makers (plant managers, CFOs, owners)
**Budget:** $500-1,000/month

**Targeting:**
- Job titles: Gerente de Planta, Director de Operaciones, CFO, Gerente General
- Industries: Manufacturing, Food Processing, Agriculture, Real Estate
- Location: Buenos Aires, Córdoba, Mendoza, Santa Fe
- Company size: 50-5,000 employees

**Formats:**
- Sponsored content (case study / ROI data)
- Message ads (direct outreach)
- Document ads (one-pager PDF)

### 3. Facebook/Instagram (Priority 3)
**Why:** Awareness, retargeting
**Budget:** $500-1,000/month

**Targeting:**
- Business owners, 35-65, Buenos Aires metro
- Interests: renewable energy, sustainability, industry
- Lookalike from website visitors

**Formats:**
- Carousel (before/after, ROI steps)
- Video (roof installation timelapse)
- Lead form (instant form)
