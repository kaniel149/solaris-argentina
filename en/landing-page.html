Financing

Options that fit your business

You don't need to invest everything at once. Choose the option that best suits your cash flow.

💡 The monthly payment is less than your current electric bill

Direct Purchase

Highest ROI

Maximum return on investment. The system is yours from day one.

  • Highest long-term ROI
  • Accelerated fiscal depreciation
  • No monthly financing costs
  • Payback in 4–6 years

Leasing — EcoLeasing

Banco Comafi

Bank financing where the payment is less than what you pay today for electricity.

  • Payment lower than your current bill
  • No significant upfront investment
  • Savings from month one
  • Ownership at end of lease

PPA — Power Purchase Agreement

$0 Investment

Zero upfront investment. You only pay for the energy you consume, at a rate lower than the grid.

  • No upfront investment
  • Predictable fixed rate
  • No financial risk
  • Ideal for companies with limited CAPEX
