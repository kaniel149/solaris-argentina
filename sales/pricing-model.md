## Revenue Streams

### 1. Project Revenue (one-time)
- Equipment markup: 15-20%
- Installation management: 10-15%
- Engineering fee: flat fee
- **Average project revenue: $15,000-60,000**

### 2. Recurring Revenue
- Solar OS monitoring: $50-150/month per site
- Maintenance contracts: $100-300/month per site
- Performance guarantee: included in monitoring
- **Average recurring per site: $150-450/month**

### 3. Financing Commissions
- Bank referral fee (Comafi EcoLeasing): TBD (typically 1-2% of loan)
- PPA facilitation: management fee on energy sold

### 4. I-REC Revenue
- Help clients register for I-RECs via IRAM
- Service fee for registration + trading
- Estimated value: $1-3 per MWh
