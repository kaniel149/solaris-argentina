# Ideal Customer Profile — Solar Argentina

---

## Primary Segment: C&I (Commercial & Industrial)

### Tier 1 — Large Industrial (500+ kWp)
- **Who:** Factories, warehouses, cold storage, food processing (e.g., fruit packing in Río Negro)
- **Roof:** 2,000+ m² flat or trapezoidal sheet metal
- **Electricity bill:** $5,000-50,000+/month
- **Decision maker:** Plant manager, CFO, or owner
- **Motivation:** Cost reduction (40-70%), energy independence, ESG compliance
- **Regions:** Buenos Aires Province (Tres de Febrero, Quilmes), Río Negro, Mendoza, Córdoba
- **Deal size:** $150,000-500,000+
- **Financing:** Bank loan, leasing (Comafi EcoLeasing), or PPA

### Tier 2 — Medium Commercial (100-500 kWp)
- **Who:** Supermarkets, shopping centers, hotels, office buildings, agricultural facilities
- **Roof:** 500-2,000 m²
- **Electricity bill:** $2,000-10,000/month
- **Decision maker:** Owner or general manager
- **Motivation:** ROI, rising tariffs, green image
- **Regions:** AMBA (Buenos Aires metro), Córdoba, Santa Fe, Mendoza
- **Deal size:** $50,000-150,000
- **Financing:** Leasing, cash, or split payments

### Tier 3 — Small Commercial (20-100 kWp)
- **Who:** Restaurants, small offices, SMEs, farms
- **Roof:** 100-500 m²
- **Electricity bill:** $500-2,000/month
- **Decision maker:** Owner
- **Motivation:** Savings, independence
- **Deal size:** $15,000-50,000
- **Financing:** Cash or installments

---

## Secondary Segments

### Real Estate / Developers
- Large roof portfolios (multiple buildings)
- ESG requirements from international tenants
- LEED/BREEAM certification
- **Key contacts:** Companies like Azrieli, Amot, Melisron (from Mivne leads)

### Agricultural / Agribusiness
- Irrigation systems, cold chain, packing plants
- Remote locations with expensive grid power
- Ground-mount + rooftop combination
- **Regions:** Río Negro, Mendoza, Tucumán, Salta

### Municipal / Government
- Public buildings, schools, hospitals
- FODIS funding eligible
- ESG/sustainability mandates
- **All 54 regional councils** = potential clients

---

## Disqualifiers (NOT our target now)
- Residential (<20 kWp) — too small, low margins
- Off-grid / rural (unless large agri) — complex logistics
- Utility-scale (>5 MWp) — different business, heavy competition
- Clients with <3 years of business — financing risk

---

## Geographic Priority
1. **Buenos Aires Province** — highest load, most C&I, best access
2. **Córdoba** — active C&I PPA market
3. **Mendoza / San Juan** — highest irradiation, established solar
4. **Río Negro** — agribusiness (fruit packing, active project)
5. **Santa Fe** — industrial base

## Key Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive upfront" | Leasing via Comafi = pay less than electricity bill |
| "Inflation will eat ROI" | Investment in USD, savings indexed to rising tariffs |
| "Regulation is uncertain" | Ley 27.191 framework exists, FODIS active, I-RECs tradeable |
| "We don't trust Chinese equipment" | Jinko = #1 global, 30-year warranty. SolarEdge/Huawei available |
| "We need to think about it" | Tariffs go up every quarter. Every month you wait = money lost |
| "Can we see a reference?" | 500+ installations in Israel, active in Panama, Solar OS demo live |
