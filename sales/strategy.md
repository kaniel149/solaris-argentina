# Sales Strategy — Solaris Argentina

---

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Anchor Projects (Month 1-2)
**Goal:** Close 2-3 projects to establish local track record

**Actions:**
1. Close Río Negro fruit packing plant (500 kWp, Intermepro quote ready)
2. Convert SWH meeting into referral partnership or pilot
3. Advance Comafi financing partnership (EcoLeasing for our clients)
4. Leverage Ruben's network for warm introductions

**Target revenue:** $45,000-90,000 in project margins

### Phase 2: Pipeline Building (Month 2-4)
**Goal:** 20+ qualified leads in pipeline

**Channels:**
1. **Ruben's network** — warm intros to industrial contacts
2. **LinkedIn outreach** — target plant managers, CFOs in Buenos Aires industrial zones
3. **Comafi co-marketing** — joint webinar/event on EcoLeasing + solar
4. **SWH referrals** — their industrial clients
5. **Cold email** — using outreach sequences to industrial zones
6. **Google Business** — local SEO for "energía solar industrial Argentina"

**Target:** 20 qualified meetings, 5 proposals

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 4-8)
**Goal:** Systematic sales engine

**Actions:**
1. Hire local sales rep (commission-based initially)
2. Launch paid ads (Google + LinkedIn, $2,000-5,000/mo)
3. Build referral network (electricians, architects, engineering firms)
4. Partner with 2-3 banks beyond Comafi
5. Attend industry events (Intersolar South America, etc.)

**Target:** 3-5 projects/month, $20,000-50,000 monthly margin

## Sales Process

### Step 1: Lead Qualification (Day 0)
- Roof size > 500 m²?
- Electricity bill > $2,000/month?
- Decision maker accessible?
- Creditworthy for financing?

### Step 2: Site Assessment (Day 1-3)
- Google Earth / satellite roof analysis
- Electricity bill review (3 months)
- Consumption pattern analysis
- Preliminary system sizing

### Step 3: Proposal (Day 3-7)
- Custom proposal via generator
- ROI calculation specific to their tariff
- Financing options (cash, Comafi EcoLeasing, PPA)
- Solar OS demo

### Step 4: Negotiation (Day 7-14)
- Address objections (use playbook)
- Adjust system size / financing
- Introduce Comafi contact if financing needed

### Step 5: Contract (Day 14-21)
- EPC contract signature
- Deposit collection (40%)
- Engineering kickoff

### Step 6: Execution (Week 3-10)
- Engineering + permitting (2-3 weeks)
- Equipment procurement (Intermepro, 2-4 weeks)
- Installation (1-3 weeks depending on size)
- Commissioning + Solar OS setup

### Step 7: Post-Sale
- Solar OS monitoring activation
- Monthly maintenance contract
- I-REC registration
- Referral request

## Pricing Strategy

### Positioning: Premium but Fair
- Not the cheapest — we include monitoring, warranty, professional service
- Not the most expensive — we're competitive on equipment (Intermepro pricing)
- **Value prop:** Israeli expertise + technology + local support

### Price Points
| Size | $/W to client | Includes |
|------|--------------|----------|
| 50 kWp | $0.65-0.75 | Equipment, install, commissioning, 1yr monitoring |
| 100 kWp | $0.60-0.70 | Same |
| 200+ kWp | $0.55-0.65 | Same + engineering |
| 500+ kWp | $0.52-0.60 | Same + project management |

### Recurring Revenue
- Solar OS: $50-150/month per site
- Maintenance: $100-300/month per site
- I-REC facilitation: service fee per registration
