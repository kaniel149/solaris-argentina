Regulatory Framework

ProgramDescriptionStatus
Law 27.191 Renewable Energy Promotion Regime. Target: 20% renewable by Dec 2025. Tax benefits: accelerated depreciation, VAT refund, tariff exemptions. EXPIRED Dec 2025. Industry seeking 12-month extension.
RenovAr Government auction program. 1,754 MW solar awarded across 4 rounds. 20-year PPAs with CAMMESA. World Bank/FODER guarantees. COMPLETED No new rounds planned.
MATER Renewable Energy Term Market. Private bilateral PPAs. 136 projects, 6,020 MW in pipeline. No subsidies — higher margins. ACTIVE Primary vehicle for new projects.
RIGI Large Investment Incentive Regime. 30-year tax benefits, international arbitration, FX access. Registration until Jul 2027. >$200M Mega-projects only. YPF Luz El Quemado approved.
RenMDI 2023 auction for 3–20 MW projects. 501 MW solar awarded. 44 projects. PPAs $52–99/MWh. COMPLETED Proven model for mid-scale.
Res. 400/2025 Mandatory 75% forward contracting. Recognizes BESS. Enables spot market for renewables. ACTIVE Drives PPA demand.

How Does It Work?

  • Private bilateral PPAs — no government subsidies
  • Generators contract directly with large consumers
  • Consumers with demand >300 kW
  • Full MATER: 3,727 MW with no injection limits
  • Referential A: 2,293 MW (up to 8% curtailment)
  • Res. SE 21/2025: generators can contract freely

Who Participates?

  • Mining: lithium in NOA/Cuyo — constant clean energy demand
  • Industrial: cost reduction and ESG compliance
  • Banks: financial sector entering MATER
  • Commercial: large retailers and chains
  • 39% of solar capacity contracted via MATER
  • Res. 400/2025 mandates 75% forward contracting

MATER has replaced government auctions as the primary driver of the solar market. Corporate demand is driving new projects with better margins than the regulated market.

Why Solar + BESS?

  • Grid stability: Res. 400/2025 recognizes BESS
  • Dispatch priority for hybrid projects
  • Premium value: higher PPA pricing
  • Curtailment solution: stores surplus energy
  • Competitive differentiation vs standalone solar

Active Hybrid Projects

  • Solar DQD: 20 MW solar + 15 MWh BESS
  • Coral Energía: BESS integrated in pipeline
  • PCR: Participating in AlmaSADI (500 MW renewables)
  • Market increasingly requires bifacial modules >600W
  • JA Solar targeting Argentina with 680W modules (2026)

SOLARIS ARGENTINA

Market Challenges

⚡

Transmission Capacity CRITICAL

Insufficient infrastructure from solar regions to demand centers. 16 priority projects: 5,600+ km new lines planned under private concession model. Years to implement.

📋

Regulatory Gap HIGH

Law 27.191 tax benefits expired Dec 2025. No replacement. Milei government prefers market approach over subsidies. RIGI only for projects >$200M.

💱

Currency Risk MEDIUM-HIGH

Historical peso devaluation. Milei's stabilization improving but unresolved. USD-denominated PPAs partially mitigate. RIGI offers 20-100% FX access.

🗳️

Political Risk MEDIUM

Milei government pro-market but unpredictable. October 2026 elections may shift priorities. Declining but elevated inflation. Impacts local costs.

🏦

Financing MEDIUM

International financing available but cautious. Local banks limited by FX risk and inflation. RIGI improves bankability for large projects.

📦

Import Logistics LOW-MEDIUM

Equipment import works but tariff exemptions expired. Environmental permits under provincial jurisdiction — 6-18 months depending on province.
