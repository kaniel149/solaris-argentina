# Argentina Solar Market Research
## Updated March 2026

---

## Market Size & Growth
- **Market value 2025:** $4.75 billion USD
- **Installed solar capacity:** 2,483 MW
- **New capacity 2025:** 810 MW (up from 307 MW in 2024)
- **CAGR 2026-2034:** 37.8%
- **Projected 2034:** $85 billion USD
- **Distributed generation (rooftop):** <1% of total — massive untapped

## Regulatory Framework
| Regulation | Description | Status 2026 |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Ley 27.191** | Main renewable energy law (20% target) | **Expired end 2025.** Seeking budget extension |
| **FODIS** | Distributed Generation Fund (loans, guarantees) | Active but underfunded (310M ARS, 0 utilized) |
| **FODER** | Renewable Energy Development Fund | Active, budget-dependent |
| **MATER** | Bilateral PPA market for large users | 450 MW awarded |
| **Res. SE 400/2025** | Market-oriented bilateral contracts | New framework |
| **RIGI** | Large Investment Regime ($1B+) | Extended to Jul 2026, mainly O&G |
| **I-REC** | Green certificates (1 MWh = 1 I-REC) | Active via IRAM since 2021 |

## Electricity Tariffs
- Industrial tariffs rising continuously (subsidy removal since 2024)
- Buenos Aires: highest tariff area
- Break-even economics improving every quarter
- **Key selling point:** "Your electricity bill only goes up. Solar locks in your cost."

## Hardware Costs
- **Equipment + installation:** $0.49-0.50/W (Intermepro quote, Mar 2026)
- **Pure equipment:** ~$0.35-0.40/W estimated
- **Full turnkey (incl. engineering, permits):** $0.55-0.70/W estimated
- **Comparison:** Global average C&I rooftop: $0.80-1.20/W

## Financing Landscape
- **No dedicated rooftop solar banking product** exists in Argentina
- **Banco Comafi / TCC:** EcoLeasing covers solar panels (up to 100% financing)
- **FODIS:** Government guarantees (severely underfunded)
- **PPA model:** Growing for large users via MATER
- **Opportunity:** First-mover advantage in structured rooftop financing

## Green Certificates
- **I-REC:** Issued by IRAM, tradeable, proves renewable origin
- **Use cases:** Scope 2 emissions reduction, RE100 compliance, CDP reporting
- **Value:** Additional revenue stream for system owners
- **Carbon credits:** Indirect alignment, not direct offset
