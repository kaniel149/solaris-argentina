# Competitor Analysis — Solar Argentina
## March 2026

---

## Direct Competitors (C&I Rooftop Installers)

### 1. Energe Solar
- **Website:** energe.com.ar
- **Focus:** Residential + small commercial
- **Regions:** Buenos Aires, Córdoba
- **Strengths:** Established brand, local presence
- **Weakness:** Small scale, no enterprise platform
- **Our advantage:** Technology (Solar OS), international experience, larger C&I focus

### 2. SunGreen
- **Focus:** C&I installations
- **Regions:** Buenos Aires Province
- **Strengths:** Local team, established relationships
- **Weakness:** No monitoring platform, limited financing options
- **Our advantage:** Solar OS monitoring, Comafi financing partnership

### 3. Sustentator
- **Website:** sustentator.com
- **Focus:** Residential + education
- **Regions:** CABA, GBA
- **Strengths:** Brand awareness, education content
- **Weakness:** Not focused on large C&I
- **Our advantage:** Industrial scale, international track record

### 4. Solartec
- **Website:** solartec.com.ar
- **Focus:** C&I + utility scale
- **Regions:** Nationwide
- **Strengths:** Larger scale, utility projects
- **Weakness:** Less focused on SME/PYME segment
- **Our advantage:** PYME focus, financing via Comafi, monitoring included

## Indirect Competitors

### enSight (ensights.ai)
- **Type:** Monitoring platform only (no installation)
- **Scale:** 6,000+ assets, 1.6 GWp, $10M Series A
- **Market share:** ~30% Israel monitoring
- **Pricing:** $5-15K/year for monitoring
- **Our position:** We install + monitor (vertical integration). They're a potential partner OR competitor for monitoring-only deals.

### Ageera (ageera.org)
- **Type:** AI-based Energy Management System + VPP
- **Focus:** Storage + solar bundle
- **Clients:** Hatzi-Hinam, Masrik, Regba, Y Center, Kastra
- **Pricing:** Hardware + software bundle (expensive)
- **Our position:** Simpler, cheaper solution for grid-tie (no storage needed). Different segment.

### Meteocontrol
- **Type:** Monitoring + PVsyst comparison
- **Focus:** Global, utility scale
- **Our position:** They're enterprise-grade, overkill for our C&I clients

3. **International track record** — 500+ installations vs. local shops with 50-100

5. **Academy** — We train local teams, reducing dependency on imported labor
