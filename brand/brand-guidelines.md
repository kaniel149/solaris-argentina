# Solaris Argentina — Brand Guidelines
### Version 1.0 | March 2026

---

## 1. Brand Overview

**Solaris Argentina** is a commercial & industrial solar rooftop company operating in Argentina's fastest-growing renewable energy market. We serve industrial, commercial, and agricultural clients with turnkey solar solutions — from design through installation and 24/7 monitoring via our proprietary Solar OS platform.

**Parent Company:** KANIEL TORD LLC (Florida, USA)
**Sister Companies:** Navitas Solar (Israel), Solaris Energy (Panama)

**Tagline:** ENERGÍA PROPIA (Own Your Energy)

**Brand Promise:** Israeli installation expertise, cutting-edge monitoring technology, and Latin American market knowledge — making solar accessible, profitable, and data-driven for every commercial building in Argentina.

**Tone Keywords:** Professional · Confident · Data-Driven · Approachable · Expert

## 4. Language

- **External communications:** Spanish (Argentine Spanish)
- **Technical documentation:** English or Spanish
- **Code & internal docs:** English
- **Sales materials:** Spanish primary, English secondary
- **Key terms to use:** Energía solar, paneles solares, autoconsumo, generación distribuida, ahorro energético

---

## 5. Brand Voice

### Do:
- Speak with data and facts (market size, ROI, savings)
- Reference local regulations (Ley 27.191, FODIS)
- Use professional but warm tone
- Emphasize track record (500+ installations)
- Talk about technology (Solar OS, monitoring)

### Don't:
- Use overly casual language
- Make promises without data
- Ignore local economic context (inflation, FX)
- Use technical jargon without explanation
